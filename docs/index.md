## Why players use it

<div class="feature-grid">
  <div class="feature-card">
    <h3>Interactive Fretboard</h3>
    <p>Tap notes and instantly detect chords with visual feedback on a familiar ukulele layout.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <h3>Progressions and Patterns</h3>
    <p>Practice real musical context with common progressions and strumming ideas across keys.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <h3>Learning and Ear Training</h3>
    <p>Build theory confidence using interval drills, lessons, and repeatable practice flows.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <h3>Offline and Private</h3>
    <p>No ads, no tracking, no account needed. Most features work fully offline.</p>
  </div>
</div>
